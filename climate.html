Do I need a climate control self storage unit in Macclenny, Florida?

Climate control is always a safe bet when renting self-storage, especially in the Florida heat.
MCM Mini Storage recommends a Climate Controlled Unit if you are:
•Storing in self storage facility in an area prone to extreme changes in temperature.

Using self storage for an extended period of time.
•Extremely valuable or climate-sensitive items would be more protected in a Climate Controlled unit.
•Planning to visit your mini storage unit frequently? Having climate-controlled self storage would make your visit much more comfortable during the summer and winter months.

Climate-sensitive items include:
•Media and electronics – Climate Control storage protects against damage caused by humidity.
•Valuable apparel – Wedding dresses, family quilts, etc…
•Wood or leather furniture: When humidity is present, organic materials like wood or leather can be a breeding ground for mold and mildew. Climate control will protect against both long-term damage and unpleasant odors.
•Books: Humidity, mold and mildew can ruin books; climate control is highly recommended.
•Musical instruments: Climate Control storage is an absolute must for keeping your instruments in playable condition.
•Glass, metal and plastics: Climate control will protect your items from warping, melting and discoloring in the Macclenny, Florida heat.

What’s the difference between air conditioned storage units, heated storage units and climate-controlled units:
•Climate-controlled storage unit: Your storage unit is kept within specific temperature ranges for purposes of minimizing humidity within the self storage units.
•Air conditioned or heated storage unit: Cold or hot air is blown into the facility and/or unit.
•Specific temperature ranges for climate control and air conditioned/heated storage units may vary by facility, so always clarify with your store manager beforehand.
